# Kadhi — Digital Loyalty Card Wallet

Kadhi is a simple mobile app that allows users to store all their loyalty cards in one digital wallet.

The idea for Kadhi came from a very practical problem at home. My dad carries a bulky wallet filled with loyalty cards, my own wallet doesn't even have space for them and my mom often forgets or loses track of her cards. Instead of juggling multiple plastic cards, Kadhi lets users keep them all in one place on their phone.

The goal of Kadhi is to make loyalty cards **easier to access, organize, and use**, without needing to carry them physically.

## Features

- **Barcode scanning:** Quickly import loyalty cards using the phone camera.

- **Digital card wallet:** Store and access all loyalty cards in one place.

- **Simple and lightweight interface:**  Designed to be quick and easy to use when standing at a checkout counter.


## Tech Stack

Kadhi is built using a modern cross-platform mobile stack:

- **React Native + Expo** — cross-platform mobile development
- **TypeScript** — type-safe development
- **Drizzle ORM** — structured local data management
- **Zustand** — lightweight state management
- **SQLite** — local data storage
