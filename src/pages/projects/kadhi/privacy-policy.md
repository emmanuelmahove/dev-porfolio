# Kadhi Wallet Privacy Policy

Kadhi Wallet respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.

Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

## **Information We Collect**

### **2.1 Personal Information**

Kadhi Wallet does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you add to the app, including loyalty card details and images, is stored locally on your device only.

### **2.2 Camera Usage**

The app requests access to your device camera solely to allow you to scan loyalty cards. Camera data is used in real time for scanning purposes and is never recorded, stored externally, or transmitted.

## **How We Use Your Information**

### **3.1 To Improve Our Services**

- Enhancing user experience within our mobile application
- Analyzing website/app usage and trends
- Improving customer service and support

### **3.2 To Communicate**

- Responding to inquiries and customer service requests

## **Data Protection**

### **4.1 Security Measures**

Because your data remains on your device, you maintain full control over it. We recommend keeping your device protected with a passcode or biometric lock for additional security.

## **Sharing Your Information**

### **5.1 Third-Party Services**

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data because no user data is collected or stored outside your device. Kadhi Wallet does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

### **5.2 Legal Requirements**

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

## **Children’s Privacy**

Kadhi Wallet does not knowingly collect information from children. Since no personal data is collected, the app is safe for general audiences.

## **Changes to This Policy**


We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the effective date above.
